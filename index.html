The Pressing Issue

Within the U.S. alone, nearly 3.5 million people suffer from paralysis: the severe loss of muscle function throughout one's body. Not only is an average health care plan for a paraplegic's life around one million dollars, but a majority of this population is often unemployed and do not have the capability to provide for themselves.
